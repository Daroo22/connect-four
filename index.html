Connect 4 Game Instructions
    Objective:
    - Be the first to connect four discs in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).
    Gameplay:
    - Players take turns dropping red and yellow discs the grid.
    - Click a column to drop your disc into the lowest available space.
    - Alternate turns with your opponent.
    Winning:
    - Connect four of your discs in a row to win.
    - If the board fills without a winner, the game is a tie.
    Restarting:
    - Click "Restart" to start a new game.
    Enjoy and strategize!
